TELCO COMPANY PROFILE & SERVICE POLICIES

Company Overview
----------------
The company is a telecom service provider offering mobile, internet, and bundled
communication services to individual customers. The focus is on affordability,
service reliability, and long-term customer retention.

Customer Segments
-----------------
1. Individual prepaid customers
2. Individual postpaid customers
3. Long-term contract customers
4. New customers (tenure less than 12 months)

Available Plans
---------------
1. Month-to-Month Plan
   - No long-term commitment
   - Higher monthly charges
   - Flexible cancellation
   - Limited loyalty benefits

2. One-Year Contract Plan
   - Discounted monthly pricing
   - Early termination fee applies
   - Includes basic customer support benefits

3. Two-Year Contract Plan
   - Lowest monthly pricing
   - Loyalty rewards included
   - Priority customer support
   - Early termination fee applies

Internet & Service Add-ons
--------------------------
- High-speed internet add-on
- International calling packs
- Streaming service bundles
- Family or multi-line discounts

Add-ons can be activated or removed at any time for month-to-month users.
Contract users may have restrictions depending on plan terms.

Billing Rules
-------------
- Monthly charges are fixed based on the selected plan and add-ons.
- Late payments may result in service warnings.
- Auto-pay customers receive small monthly discounts.
- Price increases are communicated in advance.

Customer Support Policy
-----------------------
- Standard support is available to all customers.
- Priority support is available for long-term contract customers.
- Multiple unresolved tickets may indicate dissatisfaction.
- Proactive support outreach is encouraged for high-risk customers.

Loyalty & Retention Programs
----------------------------
- Discounts for customers with tenure above 12 months
- Special upgrade offers for contract renewal
- Temporary promotional discounts may be offered
- Loyalty benefits are not guaranteed and depend on eligibility

Retention Constraints
---------------------
- Discounts must stay within approved limits.
- No forced contract renewals.
- No misleading or hidden charges.
- Offers must align with customer usage and plan type.

Common Churn Indicators (Business Perspective)
----------------------------------------------
- High monthly charges with low perceived value
- Short tenure and low engagement
- Frequent billing or service complaints
- Month-to-month contracts without add-ons
- Limited interaction with loyalty programs

Recommended Retention Actions
-----------------------------
- Suggest plan optimization instead of price cuts
- Educate customers on unused benefits
- Offer contract upgrades when suitable
- Improve service experience through support follow-ups
- Provide personalized offers based on usage patterns

Restrictions for Recommendations
--------------------------------
- Do not promise permanent discounts.
- Do not override company pricing rules.
- Do not suggest services that are unavailable to the customer.
- Do not offer benefits outside defined plans.

End of Document
---------------
This document represents the official service structure, plans, and operational
constraints of the company and must be respected when generating customer-facing
explanations or retention recommendations.
